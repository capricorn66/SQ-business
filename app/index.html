Our Mission

Serving strata’s, commercial and retail properties since 2007, Snow Queen goes above and beyond to provide peace of mind, safety and serenity to our clients. We offer comprehensive winter services, snow removal and ice melting, and liability prevention programs to the cities of Port Moody, Coquitlam and Port Coquitlam, BC. Our contracts provide all inclusive, automatic dispatch snow removal, organic grade salting and any related winter care.
